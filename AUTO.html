Project Scope

DB-ONE is handling new and used vehicle sales, vehicle service, and parts management.

• As a dealer management system, this component supports the industry-specific sales and services processes of dealers and dealer organizations, as well as vehicle importers, authorized dealers, and OEM vendors. This includes handling new and used vehicle sales, vehicle service, and parts management.

• In addition to its multi-brand capability, its also able to manage different locations, and country-specific settings including currency and taxes, meaning that it can be deployed worldwide.

• As a dealer, you can manage an order from its creation, through its completion, and right down to its billing phase. Order control enables processes to be modeled flexibly. In this way, you can meet different requirements that arise from the OEM or due to the size of a company.
